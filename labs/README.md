Ground rules:

- **Predict before you run.** Every task asks you to guess the verdict
  first. The gap between your guess and the tool's answer is where the
  learning is.
- **Read counterexamples.** They are not error spam; they are concrete
  failing executions, with the input values that break your program.
- Finished early? Each lab has a stretch task at the end — or help the
  pair next to you (explaining is the best way to find out whether you
  understood it).
